# All Dragon Locations and How to Free Dragons

There are three dragons, _**Otr**_, _**Reginn**_, and _**Fafnir**_, captured and locked up in three different regions aross the Lake of Nine in Midgard. Read on to find out all three dragon locations, which Favors to complete, and how to free all three of them.

## Veithurgard (_Otr_)

### Otr's Imprisonment

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471381/e64899eb2b7a8cd14d413553550fd20c.png/show">
</p>

Otr can be found chained to a platform in Veithurgard. To free Otr, **complete** the Favor **Otr's Imprisonment** and **destroy** three dragon shrines scattered across the region to successfully free Otr.

#### How to Find the Dragon Shrines

##### Veithurgard Dragon Shrine #1

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471382/2d7765209ce789196fb3e71ad2f6022d.png/show">
</p>

From the dock, follow the path and climb up the stairs leading to Otr. At the top of the stairs is a wide patch of land. Turn right and head towards the ledge to find the first dragon shrine.

##### Veithurgard Dragon Shrine #2

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471384/6f17cc2160f7b14b7e188bfb8ea22227.png/show">
</p>

Starting from the [first dragon shrine](#####dragonshrine#1), head left and walk up towards the grassy hill to find the second dragon.


##### Veithurgard Dragon Shrine #3

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471397/d02bcbe6ca47c8fadb869cd44540af17.png/show">
</p>

The third dragon shrine is located inside the castle. To get to this dragon shrine, turn right past the group of draugrs praying at the tall altar and follow the path to find the castle door.

Match the runes to solve the door mechanism and get inside. Once inside the castle, head up the stairs, find the red urn and hit it to reveal the path leading to the last dragon shrine.

## Konunsgard (_Reginn_)

### The Fire of Reginn

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471400/a043239b7b8c9b9e5ea110b002d473aa.png/show">
</p>

Reginn can be found chained on a ledge around the Konunsgard Strondhold Entrance. To free Reginn, complete the Favor **The Fire of Reginn** and destroy three dragon shrines scattered across the region to successfully free Reginn.

#### How to Find the Dragon Shrines

##### Konunsgard Dragon Shrine #1

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471408/72215ee757db5933cd2e5d88520280b2.png/show">
</p>

From the ledge where Reginn is chained to, jump on the other side of the path and head towards the shrine near the sap crystal.


##### Konunsgard Dragon Shrine #2

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471410/b3ea2331c700dac71354a6fd30296350.png/show">
</p>

Head left from the [first dragon shrine](#####konunsgarddragonshrine#1) and continue along the cliff towards the cave to find the second dragon shrine.

##### Konunsgard Dragon Shrine #3

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471411/0aab195ddb11313cc8da89a4f49d0196.png/show">
</p>

Past the cave, head to the sealed door and enter the area. Continue towards the bridge and cross it, and head up to the next area to find the third dragon shrine.

## The Mason's Channel

### The Flight of Fafnir

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471413/2dfdd44e60b002e17f8f8855020f22bb.png/show">
</p>

Fafnir can be found captured in an area behind the door located at the top of The Mason's Channel. **Complete** the Favor **The Flight of Fafnir** and **destroy** the three dragon shrines inside the area to free Fafnir.


#### How to Find the Dragon Shrines

##### The Mason's Channel Dragon Shrine #1

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471421/269e689eafb2e3f213361fedd181e7b9.png/show">
</p>

As soon as you enter the area, walk up the stairs to the west of Fafnir to find the first dragon shrine.

##### The Mason's Channel Dragon Shrine #2

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471425/641f34fdd6ce5ca0271d2f970b7ddbc6.png/show">
</p>

From the [first dragon shrine](#####themason'schanneldragonshrine#1), turn right and head towards a cavern to find the second dragon shrine.

##### The Mason's Channel Dragon Shrine #3

<p align="center">
  <img width="720" height="300" src="https://img.game8.co/3471440/f7b255d3dc17c8680c4dc2d04dc0f862.png/show">
</p>

Climb up the wall inside the cavern and open the gate that leads back to the starting point of the area. Head back to the starting point and climb the wall on the right to find the third dragon shrine at the top.
